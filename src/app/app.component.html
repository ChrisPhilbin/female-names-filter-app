Filter prospect list for WIB Summit



This app will help you take an export from Salesforce and return only female names - great for Women In Business (WIB) events!

AFTER EXPORTING FROM SALESFORCE BE SURE TO FIRST OPEN THE FILE IN EXCEL AND SIMPLY RE-SAVE IT
Skipping this step will cause errors and the app will likely not work properly for you.
A couple of things to note:

  • This app will identify most female names (About 90%).
  • Keep in mind some names can be both male or female.
  • Your export MUST include the prospect's First Name.
  • Your export MUST include either the Contact ID or Email column.
  • Your export must end in .xlsx
